what do we do?

We help you check if the ingredients used in your skincare products are legit. All you need to do is upload the photo of the ingredient list of the product or paste the ingredient list so that the shady ingredients could be highlighted in red, indicating what's harmful.

How do we know something is wrong?

Our principles.

Non-INCI names

All legal skincare products need to follow of stating their ingredients according to the international nomenclature of cosmetic ingredients.

Repeatated ingredients

If an ingredient is used more than once in the list, something is surely not right!

Incorrect order

ingredients are to be arranged in an descending order of weight. discrepancy such as water lying in the middle is a red flag.

No preservatives

"No preservatives" is just a too good to be true statement for a product meant to last for months.
